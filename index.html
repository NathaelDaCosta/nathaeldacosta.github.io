Nathaël Da Costa

I am a PhD candidate in Philipp Hennig's group at the University of Tübingen, and part of the ELLIS PhD programme, co-supervised by Arthur Gretton.

I am interested in mathematically rich problems in machine learning and statistics. My research focuses on deep learning theory, the foundations of Bayesian inference, and the connections between them.

My work uses differential geometry, measure theory and other areas of mathematics.

2023-Ongoing: PhD in Computer Science at the University of Tübingen

2021-2022: MMath in Pure Mathematics at the University of Cambridge (Part III of the Mathematical Tripos)
2018-2021: BA in Mathematics at the University of Cambridge (Mathematical Tripos)
